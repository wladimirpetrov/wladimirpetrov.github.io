Current Manuscripts and Research Projects

  • Planning Multi-Agent Inspection Tours with Energy Constraints and Separation Requirements
    Manuscript based on MERL internship research. My contribution focused on ROS2/CrazySwarm2 experimental integration, SLAM-based mapping, waypoint-following implementation, RViz2 visualization, and real-drone validation for multi-agent Crazyflie inspection experiments.
  • L1-Augmented Bernstein Polynomial Model Predictive Planning for Crazyflie UAVs
    Manuscript in preparation on real-time trajectory planning and adaptive disturbance rejection for Crazyflie UAVs, with experimental validation under payload and airflow disturbances.
  • Bernstein Polynomial-Based Model Predictive Control for Autonomous Underwater Vehicles
    Ongoing research on AUV trajectory optimization and control using Bernstein polynomial parameterization, nonlinear programming, BeBOT/IPOPT, and receding-horizon control.
  • Learning-Based Dynamics Modeling and Control for Autonomous Robotic Systems
    Ongoing research direction involving RBF neural networks, sequence models, and reinforcement-learning concepts for dynamics learning, trajectory generation, and data-driven robotic control.
